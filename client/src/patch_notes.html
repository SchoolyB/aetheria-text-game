  • Basic Attribute Point Allocation:

    Adding attribute points to specific areas now affects your hero and their stats. for example if you are a warrior who only has 'physical' abilities and you put all you points into Intelligence, Dexterity, and luck you aren't maximizing the damage output or overall max health of your warrior. ***Dev Note: I feel as though this feature and future ones will make players want to be more methodical and take more time when creating their hero.***

  • Basic Inventory System (Still in active development)

    • Pickup Items

      When you come across an item in the world of Aetheria you can pick it up and add it to your inventory, if you have the space.

    • Drop Items

      Be very careful of this. Once an item is dropped it is gone forever.

    • Equip/Un-Equip Items

      Just found a fancy new sword and that will increase you damage output? Go ahead and un-equip and store that old trashy sword and equip the new fancy one, straight from your inventory!

    • Use Items (Coming Soon)

      Low on health after your last battle with an enemy? Use a health potion to restore your health!

    • Examine Items (Coming Soon)

      Want to know more about an item? Examine it to get a description of the item!
