Nadafia, a long-standing rival of Empyrea, boasts both sophistication and military might. For generations, the powerful Von Schtorm-Wielder family has held sway over Nadafia, consolidating their rule and shaping the nation's destiny.

Nadafia's society is deeply rooted in religious traditions and is home to various orders and cults that play a pivotal role in its culture and governance. These institutions not only guide spiritual matters but also wield significant influence over political affairs. The land is dotted with grand cathedrals, monastic retreats, and centers of learning, making it a beacon of enlightenment and faith.
